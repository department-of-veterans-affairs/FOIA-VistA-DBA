Subject: ONCOLOGY FORDS IMPLEMENTATION

Category:

Description:
============

 Routines have been added/modified to update the Oncology Health Summary to
 reflect changes to the FORDS (Facility Oncology Registry Data Standards)
 and to add AJCC (American Joint Committee on Cancer) Collaborative Staging.

 NOIS OVERVIEW:
 =============
 1. ISP-0105-N1143
 
    Problem:
    --------
    As Reported
    +++++++++++
    The VistA ONCOLOGY Patient Health Summary needs to be updated to
    reflect the FORDS (Facility Oncology Registry Data Standards)
    changes and the addition of AJCC (American Joint Committee on
    Cancer) Collaborative Staging.
 
    As Analyzed
    +++++++++++
    The VistA ONCOLOGY Patient Health Summary needs to be updated to
    Reflect the FORDS (Facility Oncology Registry Data Standards)
    changes and the addition of AJCC (American Joint Committee on
    Cancer) Collaborative Staging.
 
    Resolution
    ----------
    These are the modifications/additions/deletions that have been made to 
    the ONCOLOGY Health Summary:
 
    Cancer Identification
    a) The item name "ICDO-Topography" has been changed to "Primary Site".
  
    Stage of Disease at Diagnosis
    a) The item name "Size of Tumor" has been changed to "Tumor Size".
    b) The following items have been added:
        Regional Nodes Examined
        Regional Nodes Positive
  
    Collaborative Staging (NEW)
    a) The following AJCC Collaborative Staging items have been added:
        Derived AJCC T
        Derived AJCC T Descriptor
        Derived AJCC N
        Derived AJCC N Descriptor
        Derived AJCC N Descriptor
        Derived AJCC M
        Derived AJCC M Descriptor
        Derived AJCC Stage Group
        Derived SS2000
  
    First Course of Treatment
    a) The item name "Non cancer-directed surgery" has been changed to 
        "Surgical Dx/Staging Proc".
    b) The following items have been removed:
        Intent of radiation
        Radiation completion status
        Reason for no chemotherapy 
        Reason for no hormone therapy 
    c) The following items have been added:
        Boost dose:cGy
        Hema Trans/Endocrine Proc
        Palliative Care

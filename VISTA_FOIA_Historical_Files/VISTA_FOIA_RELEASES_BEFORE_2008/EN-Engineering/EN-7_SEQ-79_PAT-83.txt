 NOIS OVERVIEW:
 ==============
 1. LEX-0904-40612     Addition of AMAFMF Fund
    
    Problem:
    --------
    VACO has requested that a new fund, Medical Facilities AMAF Fund
    (AMAFMF), be added as authorized by Public Law 108-199 for the
    necessary expenses for the maintenance and operation of hospitals,
    nursing homes, and domiciliary facilities.
    
    Resolution:
    -----------
    This patch adds Fund AMAFMF (Medical Facilities) as an entry in the NX
    FUND (#6914.6) file.  This file is pointed to by the Equipment Inv.
    (#6914) file.  AMAFMF is a new fund added to the property accounts for
    VA, it is a crosswalk to the new Fund 0162, Medical Facilities.  The
    fund AMAFMF will be used in AEMS/MERS to track all accountable and
    capitalized personal property for VA.
    
 TECHNICAL:
 ==========
 The following new fund will be added to the NX FUND (#6914.6) file:
    
    NX
    FUND                                                 ASSOCIATED
    CODE    BRIEF DESCRIPTION         ABBREVIATION       FUND
    ----------------------------------------------------------------------
    
    AMAFMF  Medical Facilities        FACILITIES         0162

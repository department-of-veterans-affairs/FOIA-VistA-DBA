Subject: PRE APPT & CLINIC CANCEL LETTERS NOT PRINTING CORRECTLY

Category: 
  - Routine

Description:
============

 This patch has been created to fix a problem with the Pre Appointment and 
 Clinic Cancelled Letters. It has been found that during the processing/printing
 of these letters, if a letter is found to have a patient with a Bad Address 
 Indicator set, printing is halted for the rest of the letters.
  
  
 ASSOCIATED NOIS:
 ===============
 UNY-1004-11498  Bad Address Indicator stops following letters.
  
 Duplicate: LOU-1104-41235  Sched Letters stop after certain patient.
  
  
 PARTICIPATING TEST SITES:
 ========================
 Portland, OR
 Milwaukee, WI
 Bath, NY
  
 
 NOIS OVERVIEW:
 =============
 UNY-1004-11498  Bad Address Indicator stops following letters.
  
 Duplicate: LOU-1104-41235  Sched Letters stop after certain patient.
            MWV-1104-21879/Remedy 64553
  
 Problem:
 -------
 When printing Pre Appointment and Clinic Cancelled Letters, printing will 
 halt upon finding a patient with a Bad Address Indicator set in Patient
 file #2.
  
 Resolution:
 ----------
 Make corrections to the Pre Appointment and Clinic Cancelled Letter 
 routines to continue printing the rest of the appointment letters after
 hitting a patient with a Bad Address Indicator set. Note, the patient with
 the Bad Address Indicator will not print a letter as fixed in patch
 SD*5.3*340.

Description:
============

 The Blind Rehabilitation v5.0.27.6 application provides enhanced tracking 
 and reporting of the Blind Rehabilitation services provided by:
         - Visual Impairment Service Teams (VIST) Coordinators
         - Blind Rehabilitation Centers (BRCs)
         - Blind Rehabilitation Outpatient Specialists (BROS)
         - Visual Impairment Services Outpatient Rehabilitation (VISOR) 
 Programs
         - Visual Impairment Center to Optimize Remaining Sight (VICTORS)
  
 Blind Rehabilitation build 5.0.27.6 will be promoted to the production 
 server on Sunday, May 20, 2007 at 6 p.m. CST.
  
 INSTALLATION INSTRUCTIONS:
 =========================
 This is a Centralized Server promotion from build 5.0.26.8 to 5.0.27.6 
 local sites do not need to install anything.

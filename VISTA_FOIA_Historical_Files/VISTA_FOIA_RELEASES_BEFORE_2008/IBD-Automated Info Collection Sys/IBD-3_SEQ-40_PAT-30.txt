Subject: INPATIENT EF FOR CPRS

Category: 
  - Data Dictionary
  - Routine

Description: 
============

 In order to satisfy reminders and some of the national performance
 measures, inpatient providers need to enter information using the CPRS GUI
 encounter forms.  This patch will allow for Encounter Form Designers to 
 design and assign Encounter Forms to inpatient locations.
  
 This patch does not enhance any other reports or Manual Data Entry options
 to facilitate inpatient Encounter Form data entry.

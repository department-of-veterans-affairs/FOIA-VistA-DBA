Subject: HL7 date fix

Category: 
  - Routine

Description: 
============

  
 When placed in production the change in HL7 to call the XLFDT routine for 
 HL7 to FileMan date conversion exposed an existing bug in the HL7TFM code.
 This patch corrects that problem.

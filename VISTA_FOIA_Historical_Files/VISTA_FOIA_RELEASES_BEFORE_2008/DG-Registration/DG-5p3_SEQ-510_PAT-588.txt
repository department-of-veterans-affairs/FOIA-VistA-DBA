Subject: PTF TRANSMISSIONS

Category: 
  - Routine

Description:
============

    Data is transmitted to Austin and through Austin to IPDB-CHICAGO.  
 IPDB-CHICAGO is a discontinued system. This patch disables the 
 transmission of data to IPDB-CHICAGO.
  
  ASSOCIATED NOISes:
  ==================
  AAC-0304-70724    PTF Transmissions to Austin
  LEX-0404-40200    PTF Domain-Did It Change?
  MON-0404-51284    CENSUS XMIT NOT FINDING
                    G.PTF125-IPDB@IPDB-CHICAGO.VA.GOV
  
 ASSOCIATED E3R(s):
  ==================
  N/A
    
  PARTICIPATING TEST SITES:
  =========================
  Reno,NV
  Lexington,KY
  Roseburg,OR
  Big Spring,TX
  
  NOIS OVERVIEW:
  ==============
    Problem:
    ========
    The transmission of data to IPDB-CHICAGO needs to be disabled.
  
    Resolution:
    ========
    The TRANSMISSION ROUTERS file (#407.7) will be updated.  More
    specifically, the sub-file (#407.71) record whose DOMAIN MAIL
    ROUTER is identified as IPDB-CHICAGO.VA.GOV will have its TRANSMIT
    FIELD changed from YES to NO.

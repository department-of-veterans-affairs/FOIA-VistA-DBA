Description:
============

 This patch adds several new fields to the PAID EMPLOYEE (#450), and PAID
 PAYRUN DATA (#459) files, as well as to the Initial and Payrun downloads
 as described in Service Requests 03-634 Flexible Spending Accounts and
 03-641 Employee Express Phase II and III.
  
  
 This patch addresses the following Service Request(s):
 ------------------------------------------------------
 SR 03-634 Flexible Spending Accounts
 SR 03-641 Employee Express Phase II and III
  
  
 This patch addresses the following NOIS message(s):
 ---------------------------------------------------
 There are no NOIS associated with this patch
  
  
 Overview of the Service Requests 
 --------------------------------
 The four Flexible Spending Account related fields, and the Federal
 Employee Health Benefit Court Order Indicator field have been added to the
 DEDUCTIONS/ALLOTMENTS option of the Payroll's Employee Inquiry Menu [PRSD
 04 EMPLOYEE INQUIRY MENU] menu and to the DEDUCTIONS option of the
 Payroll's Payrun Data Inquiry [PRSD 04 PAYRUN DATA INQUIRY] menu.
  
 The Earnings & Leave Print Selection field was added to the PERSONAL 
 INFORMATION option of the Payroll's Employee Inquiry [PRSD 04 EMPLOYEE
 INQUIRY MENU] menu and to the GENERAL INFORMATION option of the Payroll's
 Payrun Data Inquiry [PRSD 04 PAYRUN DATA INQUIRY] menu.

Routine Name:

 The two Flexible Spending Account fields related to the amount deducted

 The new fields were added to this routine under the GENERAL INFORMATION 
 and DEDUCTIONS categories.  Two additional loops were added to correctly 
 parse the field stings to allow for the use of the field:increment:field 
 syntax when specifying field ranges.

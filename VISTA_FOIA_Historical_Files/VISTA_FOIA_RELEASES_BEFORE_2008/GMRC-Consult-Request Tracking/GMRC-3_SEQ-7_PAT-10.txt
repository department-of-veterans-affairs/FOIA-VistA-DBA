Subject: CONSULT LOCKING CHANGES

Category: 
  - Routine

Description: 
============

   This patch is covered by the VistA Y2K Moratorium Waiver, and may be
   installed before 12/15/1999 or after 01/15/2000.
  
  This patch will enhance locking of consult requests during update
  actions. Only one user at a time will be able to act upon a
  consult/procedure request or its corresponding CPRS order.

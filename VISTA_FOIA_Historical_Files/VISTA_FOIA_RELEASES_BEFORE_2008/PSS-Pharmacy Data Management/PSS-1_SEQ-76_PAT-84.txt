Subject: CORRECT TYPO IN FILE 54

Category: 
  - Routine

Description:
============

 This patch updates a typographical error in file 54 (Rx Consult), entry 
 NO TRANSFER. It will update all 4 lines of the text as shown in solution 
 below. The text for NO TRANSFER will be updated regardless of the entry 
 number (i.e. 20).
  
 Problem:
 ^PS(54,20,0) = NO TRANSFER
 ^PS(54,20,1,0) = ^54.1A^4^4
 ^PS(54,20,1,1,0) = CAUTION: Federal law prohibits the
 ^PS(54,20,1,2,0) = transfer fo this drug to any person <===
 ^PS(54,20,1,3,0) = other than the patient for whom it
 ^PS(54,20,1,4,0) = was prescribed.
  
 Solution: Replace text for entry NO TRANSFER with the following text (1-4)
 ^PS(54,20,0)=NO TRANSFER
 ^PS(54,20,1,0)=^54.1A^4^4
 ^PS(54,20,1,1,0)=CAUTION: Federal law prohibits the <===
 ^PS(54,20,1,2,0)=transfer of this drug to any person <===
 ^PS(54,20,1,3,0)=other than the patient for whom it <===
 ^PS(54,20,1,4,0)=was prescribed. <===

 Associated NOIS:
 ===============
 ALT-1200-20530   Report display of patient age is always incorrect
  
 This patch will correct the display of the patient age within the Medicine
 Package reports.  Several reports display the patient's current age
 instead of the patient's age at the time the procedure was done.  After
 this patch is installed the age of the patient as of the report Procedure
 date will be displayed. The routines that will correct this problem are
 MCRPEC and MCARUTL4.
  
    The options are:
      ECG Test Results     [MCFPECG]
      Echo Test Results    [MCFPECHO]
      ETT Results          [MCFPETT]
  
 ROUTINE: MCRPEC
 Description:
    This routine has been updated to display the correct AGE of the patient
    when the Procedure was done.
  
 ROUTINE: MCARUTL4
 Description:
    This routine is a utility that has the function that produces
    the patient's age based on the procedure report date and the patient's
    date of birth.  The routine will get the patient's age for procedures
    within the file range 691 through 701 excluding 697.2.

Subject: COMPLEX ORDER PROBLEM

Category: 
  - Routine

Description:
============

 After finishing and verifying a complex Outpatient IV order, the
 system was treating a new order entered afterwards as a complex
 order.
  
 This patch makes a correction to the software that cleans up 
 information from a previous order before moving on to a new order.

 REMEDY OVERVIEW
 ===============
 HD108008- Complex order problem
  
 Problem
 -------
 When an outpatient complex IV order is finished and verified backdoor,
 the information pertaining to that order is not being cleaned up.
  
 Solution
 --------
 In the routine PSJO1, the variable ON needs to be killed after the call
 is made to IVSET in the EN linetag.  In the routine PSJOEO, the 
 variable PSGORD needs to be added to the variables killed in the CKNEW
 linetag.

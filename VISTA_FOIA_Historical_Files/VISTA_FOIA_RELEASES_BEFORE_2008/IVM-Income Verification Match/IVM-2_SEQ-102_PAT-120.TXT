Subject: IVM Financial Query on MT Copay Required

Category: 
  - Routine

Description:
============

 The IVM Financial Query option [IVM FINANCIAL QUERY] currently does not 
 allow a  query to be sent to HEC Legacy for the following two conditions:
  
 Condition #1: 
  - The Means Test Status on the last income test for the veteran is
    either MT COPAY REQUIRED or PENDING ADJUDICATION. 
  - The Patient declines to give income info and has agreed to pay 
    the deductible
  - The Means Test date is greater than 10/5/99 (tests performed after
    the beginning date of Means Test Sharing)
                     
 The following message is currently being displayed for this condition:
     PATIENT IS C AFTER 10/5/99 / AGREED TO PAY - QUERY NOT REQUIRED
  
 Condition #2: 
 - The Means Test Status on the last income test for the veteran is
    MT COPAY REQUIRED
  - The Patient agrees to give income info and has agreed to pay 
    the deductible
  - The Means Test date is greater than 10/5/99 (tests performed after
    the beginning date of Means Test Sharing)
  
 The following message is currently being displayed for this condition:
     PATIENT IS C/DECLINED TO PROVIDE INCOME/AGREED TO PAY - QUERY NOT
     REQUIRED
  
 This patch (IVM*2.0*120) is updating the code to allow a query to be sent 
 to HEC Legacy for these two conditions.

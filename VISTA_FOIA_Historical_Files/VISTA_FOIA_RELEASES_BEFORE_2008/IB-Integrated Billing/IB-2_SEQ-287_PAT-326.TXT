Subject: UPDATE FOR COST-BASED AND INTERAGENCY RATES

Category: 
  - Routine

Description:
============

 This patch will install the October 2005 Tortiously Liable, Cost-Based
 and, Interagency rates into the Charge Master, with an effective date 
 of 10/01/05.

 REMEDY OVERVIEW:
 ================
 1. HD64670 ISP-0105-N0770  Update for Cost-based and Interagency
  
    Problem:
    --------
    The Chief Business Office has requested a patch to update 
    Cost-Based and Interagency billing rates. These charges are for 
    applicable care, treatment and services provided in VA facilities in 
    humanitarian emergencies or to VA employees, family members or 
    ineligibles.
  
    Resolution:
    -----------
    The post-init routine IBYPPM will add 61 Cost-Based and 
    Interagency billing rates to the Charge Item (#363.2) file.

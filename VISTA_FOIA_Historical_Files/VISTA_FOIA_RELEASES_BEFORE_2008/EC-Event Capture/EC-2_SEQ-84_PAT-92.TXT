  EVENT CODE SCREENS WITH CPT CODES Report.

  REMEDY TICKET OVERVIEW:
  =======================
  HD178356:        $ZE= <NOLINE>RUN+3^%ZTMS3
  
  Problem:
  --------
  Anytime report is queued and printed to the spooler or to the device
  (with or without queue) the START^ECSCPT1 is called but there is no 
  linetag in routine ECSCPT1
  
  Resolution:
  -----------
  Routine ECSCPT was modified to call EN^ECSCPT1 to correct the problem.

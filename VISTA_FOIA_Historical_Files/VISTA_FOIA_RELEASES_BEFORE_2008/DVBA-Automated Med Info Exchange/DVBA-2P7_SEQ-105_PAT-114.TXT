Subject: CAPRI TEMPLATE UPDATES

Category: 
  - Enhancement (Mandatory)

Description:
============

 No new functionality is being provided with this patch.  This patch 
 addresses template content redesign and defect corrections to the C&P 
 examination templates accessible through CAPRI CPWM.
  
 The following defects have been addressed in this patch for Feet, Spine, 
 Skin, Diabetes Mellitus, Hypertension, Peripheral Nerves, Respiratory 
 Disease Misc, Genitourinary, and Rectum.
  
  
 FEET
 =====
     
 The three "Right" Forefoot alignment questions on the report are 
 reflecting the responses entered for "Left" Forefoot alignment.
 To recreate:
 PE Tab
 Click on Left Foot
 Is this examination for flatfoot?=YES
 Answer the three forefoot alignment questions with Yes, No, No (Close all 
 windows related to left foot)
 Click on Right foot
 Is this examination for flatfoot?=YES
 Answer the three forefoot alignment questions with Yes, Yes, Yes 
 Preview of the report showed the "Left" forefoot alignment responses for 
 both "Left" and "Right".  
  
 SPINE
 =======
  
 Spine - If only 1 side of "Pain (pinprick)" lower extremities has a 
 value, report shows opposite side with no value
  
 To recreate: 
  
 PE tab
 Click on Neuro Exam Button
 Click on Sensory button
 Scroll down to lower extremities
 Left Pain (pinprick) = 0
 Right Pain (pinprick) - DO NOT ENTER any value
  
 Report was displaying the following:
 LOWER EXTREMITIES 
   ----------------- 
     PAIN (PINPRICK): 
       RIGHT:     / 2                  <=== S/B LEFT: 0/2 
  
 SPINE
 =====
  
 Side will now be required in order to submit in the ROM screen.
  
 SKIN
 =======
 Extent of scalp affected message keeps popping up when click done even 
 though it has been completed.
  
 To recreate:
  
 History tab choose "Scarring Alopecia" only
  
 PE tab "Extent of scalp affected" choose a %
 Complete all other areas on the History, PE, Tests, and DX tabs in order 
 to click done.
 The "Extent of scalp affected" message would keep popping up.  
  
 DIABETES MELLITUS
 =================
 Data entered in the "Other Diagnosis" area of the Diagnosis tab 
 disappears after a few seconds.
  
 To recreate:
 Diagnosis tab
  
 Does the veteran have diabetes? NO
  
 If NO is selected, then clinician should make a notation in the OTHER 
 DIAGNOSIS section.
 About 7-8 seconds after entering information into the OTHER DIAGNOSIS 
 free text section, it gets erased.
  
 DIABETES MELLITUS
 =================
 Create own object for the Diabetes Mellitus Diagnosis screen so, when 
 merged with another template, it will no longer interfere with the 
 "submit" functionality in the other template's Diagnosis screen.
  
 DIABETES MELLITUS
 =================
 When Diabetes Mellitus template is merged with GU template cannot complete
 the DP and PT pulses of the lower extremity on the GU template.
  
  
 RECTUM
 ======
 When merged with another template the "You have not pressed submit " 
 message keeps popping up if data in the document problems window has not 
 been cleared.
  
 PERIPHERAL NERVES
 =================
  
 Employment section has been added to the template under the Diagnosis 
 tab and report.
  
 HYPERTENSION
 ============
  
 Diagnosis data entered disappears when Hypertension is merged with 
 another template with "submit" functionality in the Diagnosis section.  
 New object created for the Diagnosis section so this will no longer occur.
  
 HYPERTENSION
 ============
  
 Questions were not enabled in Document Problems window
  
 The following will now be enabled when appropriate:
  
 Side effects from current treatment:
 Describe current treatments including dosages
 Choose current medications
 Other medications
 Side effects of treatment
 Describe other side effects from current treatment
  
 RESPIRATORY DISEASE, MISC
 =========================
  
 No change to template.  Created unique object for the Document Problems 
 for coding purposes.
  
 GENITOURINARY EXAMINATION
 =========================
  
 When merged with General Medical Examination the Left DP and PT Pulses on 
 the Genitourinary Examination would disappear once the Left DP and PT 
 Pulses were submitted on the General Medical Examination.  This will no 
 longer occur.
  
 GENITOURINARY EXAMINATION
 =========================
  
 When merged with Diabetes Mellitus (History tab) the text entered
 in the "Specify" areas and "Describe Other" areas of Erectile Dysfunction
 would disappear.  This will no longer occur.
  
 GENITOURINARY EXAMINATION
 =========================
  
 Employment section has been added to the GU template under the Diagnosis 
 tab.
  
 GENITOURINARY EXAMINATION
 =========================
  
 The following questions have been added to the Erectile Dysfunction panel 
 for rating purposes:  "Is vaginal penetration possible? Yes/No" and 
 "Effective in allowing intercourse? Yes/No".

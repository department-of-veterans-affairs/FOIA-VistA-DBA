Description:
============

  
  
 No new functionality is being provided with this patch.  This patch 
 addresses template content redesign and defect corrections to the C&P 
 examination templates accessible through CAPRI CPWM.
  
 The following defect is being addressed in the patch for the 
 Genitourinary Template:
  
 IS OR WAS THERE A GU NEOPLASM? question was appearing on the report under 
 the Physical Examination section even though that question does not exist 
 on the Physical Exam tab of the exam.
  
 To recreate:
 History tab
 Urinary Symptoms?=YES
 Straining to urinate?=Yes or No

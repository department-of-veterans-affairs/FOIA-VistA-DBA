Subject: DELETING OPEN UORS

Category: 
  - Routine

Description:
============

 Sites are reporting "bogus" UORs that have been deleted but show on 
 reports or other options as still "open" because the delete occurred
 without prior closing of the UOR#.
  
  
 ASSOCIATED NOIS:
 ================
  
 1) BHH-0101-42313  Deleting UORs that have not been closed
      
    Duplicates:
    EKH-0201-41742  Open UORs being deleted
  
 ASSOCIATED E3RS:
 ================
  
 N/A
  
  
 PARTICIPATING TEST SITES
 ========================
  
 Black Hills, CA
 Dayton, OH
 Kansas City, KS
 Manchester, NH
 
  
 NOIS OVERVIEW
 =============
  
 1) BHH-0101-42313  Deleting UORs without closing first
  
     Problem
     -------
  
     The site was able to complete and then delete the UORs in error.
     However, the case status is still set to open for the UORs and
     appearing on their Case Assignment Register as being open.
     
     Resolution
     ----------
  
     A modification to the routine ESPOFFDE to insure that the delete
     cannot occur until the UOR has been closed prior to the delete.

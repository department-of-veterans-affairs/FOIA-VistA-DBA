Subject: ALPHA MODIFIERS NOT SHOWING AND COMMENTS PRINTING TWICE 

Category: 
  - Routine

Description:
============

 This patch addresses 2 issues:
 1) The issue of the CPT/Procedure code non-numeric modifiers not 
    displaying on Health Summary Reports. 
 2) The double printing of the comments for Health Summary Report.

 REMEDY OVERVIEW:
 ================
 1. HD0000000095982 - Ad Hoc health summary ICD 9 non-numeric 
        modifiers not displaying on the Health Summary Reports.
        
       Duplicate:
         HD0000000105689 - Ad Hoc health summary ICD 9 non-numeric 
          modifiers not displaying on Health Summary Reports.
  
    Problem:
    --------
    In the Ad Hoc health summary, the OE (Outpatient Encounter) 
    component currently pulls only the ICD 9 numeric modifiers 
    with the CPT Procedure Codes.
   
    Resolution:
    -----------
    The Routine GMTSPXOP has been modified to allow the ICD 9 
    alpha modifiers to print on the AD HOC report, as well as
    the numeric modifiers.
  
 2. HD0000000116750 - The duplicate printing of the comments on 
               the Health Summary Reports.
  
    Problem:
    --------
    In the Ad Hoc Health Summary Report, the OE (Outpatient Encounter) 
    component currently prints 2 comments in the report 
    fields when entered.
   
    Resolution:
    -----------
    The Routine GMTSPXOP has been modified to stop the printing
    of duplicate comments In Health Summary Reports.

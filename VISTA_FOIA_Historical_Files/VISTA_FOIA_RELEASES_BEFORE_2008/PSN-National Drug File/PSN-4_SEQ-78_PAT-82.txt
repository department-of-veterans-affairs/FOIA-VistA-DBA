Subject: INGREDIENTS DO NOT DISPLAY WHEN VERIFYING MATCH TO NDF

Category: 
  - Routine

Description:
============

  When verifying matches to NDF via the option PSNVFY, the ingredients
  field is always null, no ingredients display.  This also occurs when 
  matching/verifying/merging a drug to NDF when using the option, 
  [PSS DRUG ENTER/EDIT].
  
  ASSOCIATED NOIS:
  ================
  ISB-0304-31145 Ingredients do not display when verifying match to NDF
  
  ASSOCIATED E3RS:
  ================
  N/A

  Louisville, KY
  Madison, WI 
 
  NOIS OVERVIEW:
  ==============
  1) ISB-0304-31145 Ingredients do not display when verifying match to NDF
  
     Problem:
     ========
     Routine PSNOUT kills a TMP global that it builds prior to displaying
     the ingredients.
  
     Solution:
     =========
     Modify PSNOUT routine to kill the TMP global after the information has
     been displayed.

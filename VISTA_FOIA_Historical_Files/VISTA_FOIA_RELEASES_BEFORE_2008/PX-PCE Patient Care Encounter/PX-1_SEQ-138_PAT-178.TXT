Subject: DUPLICATE HEALTH FACTORS NOT BEING SAVED

Category: 
  - Routine

Description:
============

 This patch will allow users that are updating an encounter in Patient 
 Care Encounter (PCE) the ability to use the same Health Factor (HF) on
 an encounter more than once.

  REMEDY TICKET OVERVIEW:
  =======================
  HD123650 - HEALTH FACTOR PROBLEM
   
   
   Problem:
   --------
  If a duplicate Health Factor (HF) is used in the same encounter, it is
  not being saved. Often the same Health Factor will be used more than once
  within an Encounter but the comment will be different.
  
   Resolution:
   -----------
   Allow duplicate Health Factors to be used on the same encounter.
